You may only use the WHERE clause to filter individual rows, not groups of rows.

To filter groups of rows you use the HAVING clause.
SQL> SELECT city, AVG(salary)
  2      FROM employee
  3      WHERE AVG(salary) > 20
  4      GROUP BY city;
    WHERE AVG(salary) > 20
